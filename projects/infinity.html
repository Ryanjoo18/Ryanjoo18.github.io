Description

I designed a poster for Mathematics Week 2024 exploring the concept of infinite set cardinality. The poster introduced countable and uncountable infinities, illustrating key ideas such as Cantor's diagonal argument and the continuum hypothesis to spark curiosity about the nature of infinity.
