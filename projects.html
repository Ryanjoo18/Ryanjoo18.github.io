Different Sizes of Infinity

I designed a poster for the Mathematics Week 2024 at my school, exploring the concept of infinite set cardinality. The poster introduced countable and uncountable infinities, illustrating key ideas such as Cantor's diagonal argument and the continuum hypothesis to spark curiosity about the nature of infinity.

2023

Fires and Hotspots

In this project, I analyzed fire hotspots in South Sumatra using Sentinel-2 and FIRMS datasets to identify high-confidence fire zones. I processed multi-spectral satellite imagery and used True Color, False Color (NIR), and SWIR composites to visualize smoke plumes and burned areas. I presented my findings at the Space Faculty Remote Sensing Workshop 2023.
